JEE Engineering

Joint Entrance Examination

JEE Main

India's largest engineering entrance for NITs, IIITs, and top GFTIs.

  • Eligibility: 75% in PCM (65% for SC/ST).
  • Attempts: 3 consecutive years available.
  • Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT).

JEE Advanced

The gateway to the prestigious Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs).

  • Criteria: Must be in Top 2,50,000 of JEE Main.
  • Pattern: Two critical papers of 3 hours each.
  • Difficulty: Known for deep conceptual questions.
NEET Medical

National Eligibility cum Entrance Test

Exam Pattern

The single entrance test for MBBS, BDS, and AYUSH courses in India.

  • Total Marks: 720 (Biology carries 360).
  • Time: 200 minutes of intense focus.
  • Marking: +4 for Correct, -1 for Wrong.

Eligibility Hub

Focus strictly on NCERT Biology to ace the medical race.

  • Age Limit: Min 17 years (No upper limit).
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology (PCB).
  • NCERT: The primary source for 95% questions.
